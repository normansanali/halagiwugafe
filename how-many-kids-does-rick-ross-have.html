HOW MANY KIDS DOES RICK ROSS HAVE?

Publish date: 2024-09-02

How many kids does Rick Ross, real name William Leonard Roberts II, have? That’s what fans want to know after news broke earlier in the week that Ross had welcomed a baby girl with a woman named Cierra Nichole.

Cierra Nichole took to Instagram to share the news of their newest family member, Au’mei Moon Roberts. In a heartfelt message, she expressed her love for the newborn, calling her the “3rd Beautiful Princess of the Castle.”

“Au’mei Moon Roberts 11/14 7 lbs 2 oz of Perfection. Happy 2 months to Mommies 3rd Beautiful Princess of the Castle. I love you So Much. You are sooo perfect I can’t keep my eyes off of you. These past 2 months have been the best so I had to bless the world with your presence,” Cierra shared on Instagram

Toie Roberts:
Toie Roberts, born on March 17, 2002, is Rick Ross’ first child, from his relationship with Lastonia Leviston. With Toie now having a child of her own, Rick Ross proudly stepped into the role of a grandfather in June 2022.

William Leonard Roberts III:
Ross’s son with ex-girlfriend Tia Kemp, William Leonard Roberts III, came into the world in September 2005. At 18 years old, he is rising football player and a wingstop franchise owner.

Berkley, Billion, and Bliss Roberts:
Berkley, Ross’s second daughter and first child with Briana Camille, was born in September 2017. The couple expanded their family with the arrival of two sons, Billion Roberts in November 2018 and Bliss Roberts in August 2020.

Au’mei Moon Roberts:
Au’mei Moon Roberts, the newest addition of the Roberts family, was born on November 14, 2023. Her mom Cierra Nichole confirmed that she  is Rick Ross’s child after a fan asked.

While it’s now publicly known that Rick has six kids in total, his ex Tia Kemp says he has at least 10 children. Kemp shared in a Instagram video that her ex is a father of “10 children” and “two of them not even a month old”.

Are Brit Marling and Jason Isaacs married?

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Actors Brit Marling and Jason Isaacs are not married.

Jason is married to his wife, Emma Hewitt, and together they have two children.

About Brit Marling

Brit Marling is a multifaceted talent hailing from Chicago, who has made a name in American cinema as an actress and screenwriter.

Her journey to stardom began with a series of films premiering at the Sundance Film Festival, including “Sound of My Voice,” “Another Earth,” and “The East,” where her prowess extended beyond acting as she co-wrote these captivating narratives.

A graduate of Georgetown University, Marling holds degrees in economics and studio art, showcasing the blend of intellect and creativity that defines her career.

The actress ventured into the world of television with the Netflix series “The OA,” a project where she not only starred but also co-created and wrote.

The collaboration marked a new chapter in her career, demonstrating her storytelling finesse in the streaming era.

Collaborating with Zal Batmanglij, Marling continued to make waves with “The OA,” captivating audiences with its enigmatic narrative.

The series, which debuted in 2016, received acclaim for its originality and Marling’s compelling performance.

The second season, titled “Part II,” further solidified Marling’s imprint on the small screen, garnering positive reviews.

Samantha Bartolo Missing, Help Find Missing Samantha Bartolo

Publish date: 2024-09-20

Samantha Bartolo Missing – Samantha Bartolo’s family members contacted the patrol deputies of the Delta County Sheriff’s Office on Sunday, November 12, 2023, to report that Samantha Bartolo was missing and that they had not seen or heard from her in roughly two days. The report said that Samantha Bartolo had not been seen or heard from in approximately two days. It was alleged by relatives of Samantha’s family that she had been residing in a camp trailer close to the Gunnison Gorge Conservation area, which is located off of H-75 Road east of Delta.

On the morning of November 10, 2023, Samantha was last seen heading out from the trailer she shared with her lover, David Bement.  They had been living there since the previous evening. The last time anybody saw Samantha, she was dressed in black yoga pants and a black top with spaghetti straps. Samantha has a fiery mane of hair and is 5 feet 10 inches tall. If you were in the area of H-75 Road and the Gunnison River on Friday, November 10th, and you think you may have seen a woman who fits this description, please contact the Delta County Sheriff’s Office with any information you may have.

Over the last two days, members of the Delta County Search and Rescue Team as well as deputies from the Sheriff’s Office have been conducting extensive searches in the Gunnison Gorge Area in an effort to locate Samantha. Anyone who believes they may know where Samantha is is asked to contact Delta County Dispatch at 970-874-2015 or Delta County Crime Stoppers at 970-874-8810 as soon as possible.

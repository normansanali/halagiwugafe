Niharika &amp; Chaitanya gives official statement on Divorce

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Like we have revealed already, mega daughter Konidela Niharika and her husband JV Chaitanya were officially granted a divorce other day by a Family Court in Hyderabad. While the news is out, the actress remained silent the other day, but finally, she decided to give a statement in the lines of a Hrithik Roshan and Samantha Ruth Prabhu.

Taking to her social media page, the actress wrote that she is parting ways with her husband amicably and asked people to show kindness and sensitivity over the issue.

Without any fuss, she mentioned that her new private life should be welcomed, and thanked her family as well as friends for being the pillars of support.

Chaitanya Jonnalagadda, who works in the corporate sector, is the son of Guntur Inspector General of Police J Prabhakar Rao. His absence at Niharika’s brother Varun Tej’s engagement with actress Lavanya Tripathi raised questions among netizens.

Despite the fact that these celebrities openly commented about their relationship status and shared the news of their divorce as well, some folks continue to haunt them, while the media is also interested to ask them about their divorce again and again.

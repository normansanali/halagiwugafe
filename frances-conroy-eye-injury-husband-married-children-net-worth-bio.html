Who is Frances Conroy? Wiki, Bio

Frances Conroy was born on November 13, 1953, in Monroe, Georgia in the United States of America. She is 65 years old. She is an American actress. Conroy is the daughter of Ossie Hardman and Vincent Paul Conroy. Her father was a business executive while her mother was a businesswoman. Conroy developed an interest in acting while she was a young girl.

Conroy is 1m 72cm tall and has Hazel blue eyes and blonde hair colour. Her weight stands at 67 kilograms.

After attending Dickson College in Carlisle, Pennsylvania, she became a member of the Mermaid Players and performed in school theatre shows in the year 1971-1972. She later moved to New York to study drama in the Neighborhood Playhouse. She also studied at the Juilliard School and became a member of Julliard’s Drama Division Group 6 between 1973-1977.

Frances Conroy’s Career

Frances Conroy career started in the 1970s where she performed regularly with the regional acting companies. She once made an appearance as Desdemona at the Delacorte Theatre during the production of film Othello.

Her first appearance in film production was when she acted as a Shakespearean actress in Woody Allen’s Manhattan of 1979. In 1980, she made an advanced step in the film The Lady from Dubuque.  She did several other productions since then and made good advances.

Kacey Musgraves, JoJo Siwa, &amp; More Stars Step Out for GLAAD Media Awards 2022

Publish date: 2024-08-07

The stars are stepping out for the 2022 GLAAD Media Awards!

Kacey Musgraves, Christina Ricci, and JoJo Siwa were among the many stars in attendance at the annual awards event held on Saturday night (April 2) at The Beverly Hilton in Beverly Hills, Calif.

The GLAAD Media Awards honor the media for fair, accurate, and inclusive representations of LGBTQ people and issues.

During the show, Kacey was honored with the Vanguard Award while Michaela Jae Rodriguez was honored with the Stephen F. Kolzak Award.

Good News: Playboi Carti Released From Jail!

Publish date: 2024-08-29
Playboi Carti got into some legal trouble over the weekend but it looks like the situation is behind him.The rapper was arrested on gun and drug charges on Thursday (April 2). Georgia Police stopped the rapper in his Lamborghini after they noticed an expired tag. They found three guns, 12 bags of marijuana, Xanax, codeine and oxycodone. Another man by the name Jaylon Tucker was also arrested and they were both taken to county jail.

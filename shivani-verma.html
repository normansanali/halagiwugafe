Shivani Verma (born in Pune), on May 31, 1972, better known as Brahma Kumari Shivani or Sister Shivani or BK Shivani, is a Brahma Kumaris teacher at the Brahma Kumaris World Spiritual University. She has also been conferred with the prestigious Nari Shakti Award by the President of India in 2019. For her excellence in empowering Spiritual Consciousness, she was honored with the Women of the Decade Achievers Award by ASSOCHAM’S Ladies League in 2014. In 2017, she was appointed as a Goodwill Ambassador by the World Psychiatric Association too. She conducts motivational courses through public seminars and television programs. Since 2007, she has become increasingly popular in India and among the Indian diaspora through her YouTube channel- BKShivani and for her leading role in the pay-to-broadcast television series Awakening with Brahma Kumaris, which aired daily on Aastha channel.

Benjamin Byron Davis Biography

Benjamin Byron Davis is an American actor, director, writer and acting coach. Davis has appeared primarily on television series, including Without a Trace, Criminal Minds, Gilmore Girls, Windfall, MADtv, Six Feet Under, among other series.

In addition to television, he also performs on stage. Davis directed the theatre production Awake in 2011. Davis voiced multiple characters in various Rockstar titles, including Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas, L.A. Noire, and the Red Dead Redemption games.

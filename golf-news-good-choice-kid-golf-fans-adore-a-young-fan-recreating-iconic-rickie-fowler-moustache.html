Good Choice Kid: Golf Fans Adore a Young Fan Recreating Iconic Rickie FowlerMoustache

Publish date: 2024-08-03

At the 2023 Valero Texas Open, which began on March 30 and will end on April 2, Rickie Fowler will be seen playing at the tournament. He will participate in the hopes of taking home the trophy in order for him to receive an invitation to the Masters. Fowler has always been a fan favourite and recently, on the PGA Tour’s Instagram handle, a young fan was shown admiring his star player. 

A toddler adores Rickie Fowler’s mustache

In a recent post shared by the PGA Tour on their Instagram handle, a young fan was seen adoring Rickie Fowler. He was in awe of the star golfer and had pasted a similar mustache as him.

When asked about his favorite player, the kid replied, “it’s Fowler, He’s on a Full Swing, he has a nice mustache and he’s my favorite player..”. He was asked about whose mustache he thinks is better, and the kid innocently replies, “him”. 

The post was showered with lots of love from golf fans. Comments such as “This is the best”, “The cutest!” and “Can’t go wrong looking up to Rickie Fowler. Good Choice kid”  The post came across as a fun ray of hope in the sport and left all the fans in awe of the little boy. Another user commented, “Adorable, you will have your own style boy”. The post was adored by all the golf fans and everyone simply loved it. 

Rickie Fowler and his unique golf bag for Valero Texas Open

Much of Cobra’s gear is in Rickie Fowler’s bag. He plays with a Cobra King 08 flexible wedge, a Cobra LTDX LS fairway, and a Cobra Aerojet LS driver. He uses an Odyssey putter, though, and a TaylorMade ball. 

As mentioned in Golf Monthly, Here is the list of all the items in the unique bag: 

  • Driver: Cobra Aerojet LS
  • Fairways: Cobra Aerojet, Cobra LTDX LS
  • Irons: Cobra King Tour
  • Wedges: Cobra King 08 versatile
  • Putter: Odyssey versa jailbird
  • Ball: TaylorMade TP5X Pix
  • Shoe: Puma pro adapt alpha cat

Rickie Fowler has been a delight to watch for golf fans. What are your thoughts on this adorable post shared on PGA Tour’s Instagram?

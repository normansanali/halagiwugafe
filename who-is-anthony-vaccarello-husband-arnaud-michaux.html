Who Is Anthony Vaccarello Husband, Arnaud Michaux?

Arnaud Michaux, Anthony Vaccarello's husband, is also a fashion designer who works with him in the YSL atelier. 

The couple made an announcement on his Instagram account in the spring of 2021 that they had a child named Luca. Though Anthony rarely showed his son to the public, he frequently posted pictures of him on a walk with his face hidden.

Before their marriage and working with YSL, Michaux worked for Lanvin, which was based in Paris.

He was part of a small team working on ready-to-wear fashion at the Fendi atelier in Rome. Anthony claimed that after dating, he spent all his money traveling back and forth to see him.

He was aware that he would have to make a choice at some point. As a result, he chose him. Michaux was the one who pushed him to start his line. Vaccarello had some spare cash from his Fendi job, and Michaux helped out after hours.

Vaccarello has been the creative director of Saint Laurent for nearly seven years, working alongside his husband and design partner, Michaux, and a five-person studio team.

Vaccarello and Michaux appear to be completely in sync. Vaccarello once told Vogue that no matter how many people work in the YSL atelier during the day, by the end of the day, it's always only the two of them together, alone.

Arnaud Michaux and Anthony Vaccarello First Met In Art School

Anthony Vaccarello and Arnaud Michaux first met at Brussels' La Cambre art school in 2003 and began dating right away (Vogue).

 Vaccarello, the son of Sicilian parents, had ended up there after a two-year failed attempt to study law. "Arnaud was stitching all my complicated dresses at school," he recalls of their time together.

He moved to Rome after graduation to work for Fendi for two years before returning to Brussels. "My life had become routine, and I was sick of flying to Paris to see Arnaud at Lanvin," he stated.

It was exhausting and costly, so he decided to call it quits and moved with Arnaud.

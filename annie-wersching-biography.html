Annie Wersching Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Who is Annie Wersching?

Annie Wersching was an American actress.

She was best known for her role in the television series Named 24 as FBI Special Agent Renee Walker.

In the series The Vampire Diaries, she played the part of a mother. She also had a guest appearance on the shows like Star Trek: Enterprise and Supernatural.

Annie Wersching: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

She was born on March 28, 1977, in St. Louis, Missouri, USA. She was raised in St. Louis, Missouri itself, and has an Irish ethnicity.

Her mother’s name was Sandy Wersching, Surprise! Flowers and Gifts owner at St. Louis.

She won the award for the best floral designer in 2007. She passed away in February 2011 at the age of 67 due to diabetes.

Her father’s name was Frank Wersching Jr., Surprise on Euclid Avenue founder located in the Central West End. He passed away in 1989.

She attended Crossroads College Preparatory School for her high schooling in the Central West End of St. Louis.

She had been trained in Irish dance and belonged to the St. Louis Celtic Step dancers in her youth days. From Millikin University, she has a degree in musical theater.

Annie Wersching’s Death

In mid-2020, Annie was diagnosed with cancer, which she kept private and continued to act afterward.

On January 29, 2023, she died in Los Angeles, Califonia at the age of 45.

Annie Wersching: Professional Life, and Career

Annie Wersching was working on the crime show named Bosch. Wersching started her career as an actor with a guest appearance on the show Star Trek: Enterprise.

Later, she started getting roles on such shows as Charmed, Killer Instinct, Supernatural, and Cold Case.

She played the role of Amelia Joffe which was broadcasted by ABC Daytime from March to November 2007.

After that, she bagged the role to play the FBI Agent as Renee Walker on the series 24. She was shown in seasons 7 and 8.

Wersching guest-starred on various shows, which also included CSI, NCIS, Rizzoli & Isles, Hawaii Five-0, Body of Proof, Dallas, Revolution, Castle, Blue Bloods, The Vampire Diaries, and Touch.

Apart from television series, she has acted in movies like Bruce Almighty(2003), The Showdown (2006), Below the Beltway (2010), TV movies Blue-Eyed Butcher(2012), and The Surrogate(2013). She has also worked in theaters.

Net Worth and Salary

As of 2023, this actress’s net worth was $5 million. As an actor, her average salary was around $19k-$210k per year.

Annie Wersching’s Rumors and Controversy

There were rumors when her character in the series 24 was to die, she had begged the makers not to do so.

But later in an interview, she confirmed that she was feeling sad about her part but she did not beg.

Matthew Mindler Cause Of Death

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Matthew Minder, a famous child actor, died at the age of 19. According to media reports, he went missing during the week and his death was confirmed by the University he attended.

Matthew who starred in the 2011 movie, Our Idiot Brother, was declared missing from his University in Pennsylvania late on Wednesday after failing to return to his dormitory nor answer calls from his friends and family.

TMZ reports that the law enforcement confirmed his body had been found but did not reveal the cause of his death.

In a statement issued by the University to confirm his death, they mentioned that, “It is with a grieving heart that I let you know of the death of 19-year-old Matthew Mindler from Hellertown, Pennsylvania, a first-year student at Millersville University.

A search had been underway for Matthew since Thursday, after he was reported missing. Matthew was found deceased this morning, Saturday, August 28, in Manor Township near campus.’

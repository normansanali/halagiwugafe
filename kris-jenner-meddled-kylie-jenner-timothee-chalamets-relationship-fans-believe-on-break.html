Many fans are curious to know what happened to Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet amid reports they’re on a break. The couple went from being PDA-heavy and doing red carpet appearances together at the beginning of the year, to barely being seen in recent months.

Now, a new report claims that Kylie’s mother Kris Jenner may have played a part in driving the two apart, but not even intentionally, as it sounds like the Kardashian-Jenner lifestyle may be a bit much for Chalamet.

Kris Supposedly Supported Kylie And Timothee, But Became Overbearing

Speaking to Star Magazine, a source claims that Kris was supportive of Kylie and Timothee’s romance from the get-go, as she sees him not only good for her daughter, but also for the family brand.

"Kris thinks he’s the ultimate catch," the source claimed. "He has huge star power along with this intellectual cachet, and Kris thinks he can elevate the family into a classier social category."

However, Kris may have overstepped her boundaries. The source said the momager attempted to arrange brand deals for Timothee as well as invivte him to events and family functions without consulting Kylie first, likely leading to friction in the couple’s relationship.

Why Fans Think Timothee And Kylie Are Taking A Break

It's not just fans theorizing that the couple are having relationship problems. Reports claim the two are on a “break” as they try to sort things out.

"They’re hardly together anymore,” a source told OK! Magazine. “Their lives got busy, and they drifted apart. Of course, once their schedules quiet down, that could all change."

This supports other reports that have suggested Timothee was worried how his relationship with Kylie was affecting his career, particularly after rumors flew at the Golden Globes that they’d had drama with Selena Gomez.

Illenium Debuts Upcoming Seven Lions Collab

Publish date: 2024-07-29

During his live set at Das Energi Festival in Salt Lake City, Utah, Illenium debuted a massive upcoming collaboration with none other than Seven Lions. Fans are understandably beyond excited to see the two producers pair up on a joint effort, which has also been confirmed to feature Denver based artist Said The Sky. Luckily, a fan captured a video clip of the performance, and it’s now hit the internet for streaming to give us our first taste of the forthcoming offering.

Recommended Videos

The ID in question features a classic melodic dubstep sound reminiscent of Seven Lion’s earlier work, opening with angelic female vocals over a celestial chord progression. Rising percussion builds the track up into the drops, with blaring basslines, half time beats and cascading melodies rounding things out.

Seven Lions had previously delivered a remix Illenium’s “Fortress,” and a collaboration between the two artists is a match made in heaven that we can’t wait to hear more of. There’s no official release date at this point for the track, but stay tuned for more updates as they become available.

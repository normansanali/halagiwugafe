Who Is Nima Momeni? Suspect in The Murder of Cash App Founder Bob Lee Is Apprehended!

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Thursday morning, the San Francisco Police Department made an arrest in the stabbing death of Cash App co-founder Bob Lee. According to the San Francisco County Jail’s records, the detained suspect is Nima Momeni, a fellow tech executive who allegedly knew Lee.

The San Francisco Police Department has identified Momeni, 38, of Emeryville as the murder suspect. Thursday morning, Momeni was booked on a single count of homicide, according to jail records. According to the inmate record, Momeni is currently being held without parole.

Who Is Nima Momeni?

According to his LinkedIn page, Mr. Momeni is the owner of Expand IT and resides in Emeryville in the Bay Area. The corporate software company may rely on Expand IT as their “one-stop technology and security provider.”

According to his resume, Mr. Momeni has worked as a “dedicated technology partner” for several technology firms in the Bay Area and San Jose since 2005, assisting clients in the healthcare industry, the financial sector, and the startup community.

His profile states that he is bilingual (English and Farsi) and a graduate of Berkeley University. Mr. Momeni paid $525,000 in 2020 to purchase an apartment at 4053 Harlan St., Emeryville, according to public records searches.

What Happened to Bob Lee?

Technology executive Robert Lee was fatally murdered on April 4, 2023. Mission Local reports that his body was found in a forsaken part of San Francisco.

The two men apparently knew each other, so this wasn’t a random attack or a robbery, say police sources. On the morning of April 4, they were supposedly walking through downtown San Francisco. The car’s name was Momeni.

“A confrontation apparently started while both men were in the car and possibly continued after Lee got out.” Mission Local reports that his body was found in a forsaken part of San Francisco.

Who Was Bob Lee?

American entrepreneur and software engineer Bob Lee founded Cash App. He served as Square’s chief technology officer and MobileCoin’s chief product officer.

Lee was born on December 20, 1979, in St. Louis, Missouri, to Rick “Ricky” Lee. Brother Tim Oliver Lee is his. Following the passing of Lee’s mother in 2019, Rick relocated to Mill Valley, California to reside with Lee’s family. In October 2022, Bob Lee and his father relocated again to Miami.

During his time at Lindbergh High School, Lee coded a 3D visualization engine in Turbo Pascal and earned the nickname “Crazy Bob” for his unbridled enthusiasm for water polo. Lee attended Southeast Missouri State University, where he became a member of Sigma Chi. Former Missouri House of Representatives Speaker Rob Vescovo was one of Lee’s fraternity brothers.

Early Life

Mac’s full name was Malcolm James McCormick. He was born on January 19, 1992, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. he was an American by nationality. He is currently 30 years old. Likewise, he was born to parents Karen Meyers(Mother) and Mark McCormick(Father). His mother Karen was a professional photographer by profession while his father was an architect.

Both his parents belong to two different faiths. His mother is a Jew and his father practices Christianity. Likewise, he had an older brother whose name was Miller McCormick. Talking more about his childhood, he was into music from a very young age. He started playing a lot of different instruments when he was only six years old.

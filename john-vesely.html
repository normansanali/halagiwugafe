Biography

John Vesely was born in California on February 5, 1982. American musician best known as the frontman for the acoustic rock band Secondhand Serenade. He played on their 2007 debut album, Awake, and their 2008 follow-up, A Twist in My Story. His second album, A Twist in My Story, recounted his devastating divorce from Candice; it was produced by Butch Walker. He played in several bands beginning when he was twelve that encompassed several genres of music, including ska, hardcore, rock, and pop.

How Old Do You Have to Be to Work at Five Below?

Five Below is a popular discount store that offers a range of products including electronics, clothing, furniture, toys, and many others. The company has more than 1,000 stores in over 30 states across the United States, which makes it a popular destination for job seekers.

If you’re interested in working at Five Below, one of the first questions you may have is “How old do you have to be to work at Five Below?” In this article, we’ll provide the answer to this question and provide you with some frequently asked questions about working at Five Below.

What is the minimum age requirement to work at Five Below?

The minimum age requirement to work at Five Below is 16 years old. However, the company reserves the right to set a higher minimum age requirement in certain states where the legal working age is higher.

What positions are available for 16-year-olds at Five Below?

If you’re 16 years old, you can apply for an entry-level position at Five Below, which may include roles such as:

  • Sales Associate
  • Cashier
  • Merchandise Handler
  • Stock Associate
  • Customer Experience Specialist

What availability will be required for working at Five Below?

Five Below requires its employees to have flexible availability. In most cases, you’ll need to be available on weekends and evenings as these are typically the busiest times for the store.

How much does Five Below pay its employees?

At Five Below, the hourly pay for entry-level positions varies depending on the state, but the company pays competitive rates. On average, you can expect to earn $9-$10 per hour.

Do Five Below employees get employee discounts?

Yes, Five Below employees receive a 10% discount on all purchases.

What benefits does Five Below offer its employees?

Five Below offers a range of benefits to its employees, which include:

  • Flexible Schedules
  • 401(k) Retirement Plan
  • Medical, Dental, and Vision Insurance
  • Paid Time Off

Is there room for advancement at Five Below?

Yes, Five Below offers plenty of career advancement opportunities for its employees. You can advance into a supervisory, management, or corporate role with the company.

What is the work culture like at Five Below?

Five Below has a positive work culture, which is focused on creating a fun and interactive experience for its customers and employees. The company values teamwork, integrity, and innovation, and it strives to provide a positive work environment for its staff.

What training does Five Below offer its employees?

Five Below provides training to its employees to help them understand the products, customer service expectations, and management onboarding that is expected. The company also provides ongoing development programs for employees who want to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Does Five Below offer internships?

Yes, Five Below offers internship programs for college students to gain experience in the retail industry and to learn about the company’s culture and values.

What is the hiring process like at Five Below?

The hiring process at Five Below is very straightforward. You’ll first need to submit your application through Five Below’s career website. If your application passes, you’ll be invited for a job interview.

Do I need prior experience to work at Five Below?

No, prior experience is not required to work at Five Below. The company provides on-the-job training for new hires.

What should I wear for a job interview at Five Below?

For a job interview at Five Below, dress nicely but avoid wearing anything too formal. Men should wear dress pants and a collared shirt, while women can wear dress pants or a skirt with a blouse. Additionally, you can wear accessories and jewelry, but keep it minimal and avoid wearing anything too flashy.

Does Five Below conduct drug tests for new employees?

Yes, Five Below conducts drug tests as part of its hiring process. All new employees are required to undergo a drug test and pass it before starting work.

What should I expect on my first day of work?

On your first day of work, you should expect to receive an orientation that provides an overview of the company, store policies, and procedures. You’ll also receive training on how to operate the register, the layout of the store, and merchandise handling.

What qualities does Five Below look for in its employees?

Five Below looks for employees who are passionate, energetic, enthusiastic, and have a strong work ethic. The company values employees who are friendly, personable, and have excellent communication and customer service skills.

In conclusion, if you’re 16 years old and looking for a job in the retail industry, Five Below may be a great option for you. The company is a popular destination for job seekers and offers a range of positions that pay well and provide great benefits.

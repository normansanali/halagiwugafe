toile Labrine, Cascade, Seychelles

Publish date: 2024-07-14
Etoile Labrine is a new establishment on La Digue island.
La Digue being the 4th largest island is very popular for its well known beaches such as Anse Source d’Argent, Grand Anse and Anse Marron.
Situated at L’Union, Etoile Labrine is the nearest guest house to the famous Anse Source d’Argent beach.
To explore the beauty of La Digue Island guided tours to secluded spots like Anse Marron, Anse Coco and other beaches are organized by the guest house.
As the main means of transport on La Digue is the traditional bicycle, rentals can be arranged at the reception.

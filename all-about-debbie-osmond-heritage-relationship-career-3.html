Is Debbie Osmond Married? Relationship

Debbie and Donny married after a long romance on May 8, 1978. Their wedding ceremony was held at the Salt Lake LDS Temple in Utah. Donny’s father was actually opposed to her marriage, but they still got married.

They had been married for 43 years as of 2021, with no rumors of divorce.

Debbie dated Donny’s brother before falling in love with him.

Donny met his wife for the first time when she was just 15 years old. At the time, she was dating Jay Osmond, the brother of her current husband. Donny persuaded her to have a committed relationship with him after three years of effort. As they say, the rest is history.

They share their five children from their marriage

The couple is very proud of their five children. Their first son, Donald Clark Osmond Jr., was born in 1979 and their second son, Jeremy James Osmond, was born in 1981.

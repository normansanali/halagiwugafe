American boxer Terence Allan Crawford was born on September 28, 1987. Including the World Boxing Organization (WBO) welterweight title since 2018, he has held numerous world titles in three weight classes.

In the past, he held the WBO and Ring magazine lightweight titles from 2014 to 2015, as well as the combined titles of the World Boxing Association (Super version), World Boxing Council (WBC), International Boxing Federation (IBF), WBO, and Ring light welterweight from 2015 to 2017.

Crawford started boxing when he was seven years old. He competed in 70 official amateur fights, losing 12 of them. He rose to the top-ranked lightweight in the US shortly before the 2008 Olympics after taking first place in three amateur tournaments. However, his defeat to leading contender Sadam Ali ended his Olympic hopes.

Terence Crawford has an impeccable professional boxing record, having won 39 of 39 fights. Thirty of his 39 victories have come by knockout, with the remaining nine coming by decision.

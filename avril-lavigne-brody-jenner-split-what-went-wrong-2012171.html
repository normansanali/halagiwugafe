Avril Lavigne, Brody Jenner Split: What Went Wrong?

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Complicated, indeed.

On Monday, Us Weekly broke the news that Avril Lavigne and Brody Jenner split last week after nearly two years together.

 "It was mutual, but Brody really broke up with her," a pal explains to Us Weekly of the breakup. Jenner "started" the conversation as they were discussing singer Lavigne's upcoming tour dates in Asia. "He told her he was tired of always being her plus-one, and always going on tour with her."

Former Hills star Jenner, 26, "said he needs a career of his own again, and he didn't want to go on the tour. Avril didn't like that."

The Eagles Hotel California tour: Where are they playing and how can I buy tickets?

Publish date: 2024-09-03

FOR over 50 years, The Eagles have been a rock band sensation with no plans on slowing down anytime soon.

Over the years, the band has won six Grammy Awards and five American Music Awards and is about to embark on their latest tour named after their fifth studio album, Hotel California.

  • February 21: Spectrum Center in Charlotte, NC
  • February 24: Amway Center in Orlando, FL
  • February 25: FLA Live Arena in Sunrise, FL
  • February 28: Amalie Arena in Tampa, FL
  • March 2: PNC Arena in Raleigh, NC
  • March 4: State Farm Arena in Atlanta, GA

  • March 24: Little Caesars Area in Detroit, MI

The tour is expected to include Country Music Hall of Famer Vince Gill who has toured with the band since 2017 following Glenn Frey's tragic passing.

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for The Eagles Hotel California tour are available through their website, TicketMaster and VividSeats.

more from rock music

Prices for the tickets range anywhere from $185 to $5000, depending on the venue and seats.

A limited number of VIP packages are also available but vary again based on the venue.

Who are the members of the band?

Over the years, The Eagles have seen different musicians come in and out of their band since their debut in 1971.

For their 2022 tour, fans can expect to see Don Henley, Joe Walsh, Timothy B. Schmit, Deacon Frey, and Vince Gill on stage.

Guitarist and singer Glenn Frey passed away in 2016 at the age of 67.

At this time, Henley is the only remaining founding member of the band.

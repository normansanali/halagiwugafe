What are the Best MLS Card Packs to Buy in 2024

Publish date: 2024-07-31

The MLS card market has been gaining popularity, making it an exciting time for collectors and investors to explore the best MLS card packs in 2024. Here's a detailed overview of some of the top MLS card packs to consider for this year.

2023 Topps Major League Soccer

The 2023 Topps Major League Soccer card packs are a fantastic choice for collectors looking to dive into the world of MLS cards. This set features a 200-card Base Set, including a 25-card Pitch Prodigies subset that highlights the league's top rookie talents. The set also offers various Base Set Parallels, such as the Icy White Foil, Blue Foil, Green Foil, Icy Blue Foil, Icy Black Foil, Red Foil, and Black Foil, which cater to different preferences and add excitement to the collecting experience. Additionally, collectors can look out for the AUTOGRAPH CARDS and INSERT CARDS like Big City Strikers, Kitcore, and Topps 1983 Futbol, each offering unique and limited editions to enhance any collection.

2023-24 Futera Unique World Football

The 2023-24 Futera Unique World Football card packs offer collectors the chance to acquire unique and high-quality cards featuring the stars of soccer. With one hit per pack and four hits per box, this set provides an exclusive and thrilling collecting experience. The cards in this set are designed to appeal to soccer enthusiasts and collectors alike, making it a valuable addition to any collection.

2023-24 Topps Now Bundesliga

For those interested in the German soccer league, the 2023-24 Topps Now Bundesliga card packs are a must-have. This print-to-order set captures the standouts of the league each week, offering a unique and dynamic collecting experience. The set features Red and Black Parallels, adding an element of rarity and exclusivity to the cards.

2023-24 Topps Major League Soccer 11-Pack Blaster Box

The 2023 Topps Major League Soccer 11-Pack Blaster Box is an excellent choice for collectors seeking an assortment of MLS cards. This value box includes 4 Exclusive Icy Foil Parallels, allowing collectors to expand their collection with unique and eye-catching cards. The set also boasts a 200-card Base Set and various Base Set Parallels, as well as INSERT CARDS like Big City Strikers, Kitcore, and Topps 1983 Futbol, offering a diverse range of cards to collect and enjoy.

2024 MLS Cards

As the MLS card market continues to evolve, collectors and investors can look forward to the release of the 2024 MLS cards. With the ongoing growth and popularity of MLS, these upcoming card packs are expected to offer innovative designs, valuable inserts, and sought-after parallels, making them an exciting prospect for collectors in 2024.

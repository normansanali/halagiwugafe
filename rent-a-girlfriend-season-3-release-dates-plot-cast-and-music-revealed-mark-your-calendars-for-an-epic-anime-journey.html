Get Ready for Romance and Drama: Rent a Girlfriend Season 3 Plot Revealed!

So, what’s Season 3 all about, you ask? Well, get ready for some serious heart-fluttering moments! Kazuya and Chizuru are teaming up to create a movie that will melt your heart. But it’s not just any movie – they’re crowdfunding it to fulfill Chizuru’s promise to her sick grandma. Can you say “awww”?

But hold on tight, because things won’t be easy for our lovebirds. They’ll face intense challenges from other ladies in Kazuya’s life and a wild card arrival named Mini Yaemori. Will their fake relationship turn into a real deal? You’ll need tissues for this emotional rollercoaster!

The Talented Cast and Catchy Music of Rent a Girlfriend Season 3: Here’s What You Need to Know!

Let’s talk about the cast, shall we? The dream team is back with-

  • Shun Horie as Kazuya Kinoshita
  • Sora Amamiya as Chizuru Mizuhara
  • Rie Takahashi joins as Sumi Sakurasawa
  • Aoi Yuki as Mami Nanami
  • Nao Toyama as Ruka Sarashina
  • Yu Serizawa as the sassy Mini Yaemori

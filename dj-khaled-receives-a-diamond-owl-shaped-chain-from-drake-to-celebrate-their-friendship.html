DJ Khaled Receives A Diamond-Owl Shaped Chain From Drake To Celebrate Their Friendship

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Canadian rapper, Aubrey Drake Graham, simply known by his stage name as Drake has demonstrated an act of love and kindness to Music producer and DJ, Khaled Mohamed Khaled, better known as DJ Kahled as he gifts him a diamond-owl shaped chain to celebrate their friendship.

DJ Khaled took to his Instagram to share a video of the expensive chain. He captioned the video;  “Thank you @champagnepapi for this beautiful gift. Luv forever. #WETHEBEST #OVO Luv always KHALED KHALED.”

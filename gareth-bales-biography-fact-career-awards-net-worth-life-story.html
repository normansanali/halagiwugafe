Welsh football player Gareth Bale has an estimated net worth of $90 million in 2015. He is currently playing for Real Madrid of the La Liga Club. He is also the winger of the Welsh national team.

Gareth Frank Bale was born on July 16, 1989 in Cardiff, Wales, United Kingdom. He started off his professional football career with the Southampton. Eventually, he moved to Tottenham Hotspur which he played for from 2007 through 2013.Bale is known for his impressive left foot, great crossing ability, tremendous speed, and exceptional physical qualities. In 2011 and 2013, Bale was named as the PFA Players’ Player of the Year. In 2011, he was named as the UEFA Team of the Year. During the 2012–13 season, he became a recipient of numerous awards which include the PFAPlayers’ Player of the Year, FWA Footballer of the Year and PFA Young Player of the Year awards.

On September 1, 2013, he moved to Real Madrid for an undisclosed amount. It was reported by the Spanish press that the transfer fee was around £77 million (€91 million) while the English press claimed that the transfer fee was estimated to be around £85.3 million (€100 million). The amount reported by the English press is way higher than the transfer fee of Cristiano Ronaldo which was estimated to be around €80 million (€94 million).

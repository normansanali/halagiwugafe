Serena Pitt News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Name:Serena Pitt

Birthday:October 25, 1997

About

Born in Ontario, Canada, Serena Pitt gained an education at Wilfrid Laurier University in communications and business management. She later joined a certificate program at George Brown College. After completing an internship with the MacIntyre Communications firm, Pitt appeared on season 25 of The Bachelor. After quitting her attempt to win Matt James‘ heart, the contestant returned to the Bachelor franchise during season 7 of Bachelor in Paradise.

The publicist found herself forming a connection with Joe Amabile, but the couple hit a snag when the Chicago native’s ex Kendall Long appeared in Mexico as well.

Pitt and Amabile reached a major milestone in their relationship when the grocer got down on one knee during the season 7 finale in 2021. Pitt married Amabile in October 2022.

Brundage Mountain Resort receives season's first snowfall

Publish date: 2024-09-03

MCCALL, Idaho (CBS2) — According to a recent news release, The season's first snow has fallen at the Brundage Mountain Resort summit. An overnight storm dropped about an inch of snow, and the precipitation came down as rain in the base area.

"This first blast of snow is more symbolic than anything," says resort spokesperson April Whitney. "We don't expect it to stick around long, but even a little blanket of white on the mountain gets our snow fanatics excited."

The forecast includes a slight chance of more snow overnight Wednesday, but daytime temperatures are expected to be well above freezing. Snow Report and Forecast

This is the first snowfall recorded at Brundage since the official start of the 'snow year,' which was Oct. 1. October snow is not unusual, but it is rare for snow that falls in early October to stick around for the whole season due to temperature fluctuations.

Wiki/Biography

Anna-Maria Sieklucka was born on Sunday, May 31, 1992 (age 28 years; as in 2020), in Lublin, Poland. She grew up in Wrocław. Her zodiac sign is Gemini. Anna did her schooling from II Liceum Ogólnokształcące im. Hetmana Jana Zamoyskiego, Lublin. She studied at AST National Academy of Theatre Arts in Wrocław-based faculty and graduated in 2018.
